Kell Golden

I'm an aspiring Product Manager that is looking for an opportunity to solve the world's most complex problems using cool products. Moreover, I desire to use my professional experiences to socially innovate the way businesses and teams operate, through the lens of creativity, diversity, and integrity. After being in campus ministry for 5 years, I decided to make a career pivot towards working with disrupting technologies as a Product Manager. Knowing then, that I lacked the skills and knowledge in this field, I volunteered and freelanced at multiple start-ups and tech companies to gain some product-related experiences. Due to my efforts wearing multiple hats in this ecosystem, I was able to grow in empathy. Working cross-functionally, gave me an intimate view of what intrinsically motivates team members (words of affirmation, great communication, full-transparency – even if it's inconvenient). In previous roles, working on many agile development teams as either a product marketer, software trainer, or business developer, I have had the opportunity to create user web-flows for new product releases and have created software training processes that increased operational workflow by 50%.
